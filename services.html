Intergrace Pty Ltd and our consulting arm, Leoy Solutions Pty Ltd, provide services in enterprise application development and integration. We develop and support multi-tier software solutions. We do not resell any hardware or software, so we are completely impartial in our solutions to the clients.

Our developers have over 20 years of experience in a variety of programming languages and platforms, including Java/J2EE, Kotlin, Android, Go (Golang), Javascript (including ES6+), Typescript, React, Angular (1 and 2+), Node.js, C#/.Net, C/C++, Perl, PHP and more.

Over the years we helped many companies developing their enterprise systems and always learnt something new along the way.

We are equally versed with the inexpensive in-house solutions and the highly scalable cloud-based (AWS, Google Cloud, OpenShift, Azure, Heroku, etc) containerised (Docker) platforms.

We are confident that our broad experience is our great advantage.
