Welcome to Intergrace Pty Ltd, the no-nonsense software company! Here at Intergrace we don't tell our clients to buy ridiculously expensive hardware, database or middle-tier software in order for our products to be deployed. We provide high-performance, scalable and fault-tolerant solutions that can be deployed on any existing application servers, including free/open source ones such as Tomcat and JBoss.

We provide our expertise in development, integration and support of the high quality enterprise applications for financial, insurance, telecommunication and other industries.

We have extensive experience in a broad variety of programming languages, protocols, operating systems, application servers, databases and middle-tier systems.

We are equally happy to work on a small one-person one-week project or on a large-scale big team major corporate activity. Our team is dynamic, highly motivated, agile and enthusiastic. Try us out, we promise you'll never regret it!
